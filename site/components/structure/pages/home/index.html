What makes design strong and perfect is uniformity: a few design elements used and reused all over again.

Fonts, headings, links, colors, navigation, lists, backgrounds, separators, forms, images—we have already ten elements all mandatory parts of a website. You can use a couple of font families, you have to have at least three headings, you need at least a couple of colors, you have more than one navigation block, and multiple backgrounds.

From ten basic design elements you are already over twenty or thirty at least. People can focus on five content blocks at once—this might be true also for design elements.
